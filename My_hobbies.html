Hobbies and Career

From my early childhood I was playing basketball and even attended the training camp of under 16 of our national team

My passion for basketball has ended and I focused fully on studies

After my freshman year I went to London and got a job in a busy bar located in Covent Garden - suddenly fell in love with it.

Since then I have worked in 2 high end bars in London and got a lot of experience.

After that I went to the states and worked in Ocean City, Maryland.

Now I work as the head bartender in my hometown at Irish Nese Pub and at the same time I compete in numerous championships
